Omni Marketing Cincinnati brings worldwide innovation to retail and shopper marketing. The entire spectrum of companies building the retail marketing ecosystem - retailers, technology vendors, payment processors, mobile developers, data providers, advertisers, agencies, and investors will come together for a unique, one-day event focusing on the online to offline dynamic and closing the transaction loop at retail. The day commences with an overview of emerging trends impacting retail marketing by Optimus Research who also guide participants through a series of case study centric sessions. Interspersed throughout the day technology companies introduce their solutions with specific use cases, which will be demonstrated at the Innovation Showcase the following morning. Large retailers, service providers, and innovators will interact around real world examples of emerging innovations on how to connect online and offline as well as close the transaction loop between the two. Networking opportunities will be available during breaks and at the post conference reception at the 21c Museum Hotel across Walnut Street from the Aronoff Theater conference venue. The conference is presented by Helsinki Partners and Optimus Research. It is a no charge and invite only event.
