Mark Raymond
Founder/Managing Director Mark is an experienced technologist, executive, founder, and private equity investor. He is founder/Managing Director, which manages corporate innovation and venture investment programs. Mark has been involved in many entrepreneurial ventures. He founded Symphony Communications in 1997 raising $38 million in funding from leading VCs, including Charles River Ventures, APAX Partners and GE Equity.
